About Us

We are a professional Football Team based in Munich, Germany (München) in the Bundesliga (German Football League). The team was established in 2024 by the club president Dawson Zorn with hopes of creating a championship contending team and to represent the city of Munich

News

April 5th 2024

ZornFC defeat Borussia Dortmund 2-1 away at Signal Iduna Park on their season opener!

Meet The President

Dawson Zorn Born November 5th 2002. Dawson grew up playing football throughout his youth but never able to make it to the professional stage, he now has purchased this club hoping to make a team worthy of winning the champions league and Bundesliga title.
